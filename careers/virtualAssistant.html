Job Description

(
Virtual Assistant
)

As a Virtual Assistant, you will provide crucial administrative support to help streamline operations and enhance productivity for our clients. This remote position is perfect for detail-oriented individuals who excel in multitasking and possess strong communication skills.

You will play a vital role in ensuring that daily tasks and projects run smoothly.

Job Responsibilities

  • Administrative Support: Provide general administrative assistance, including scheduling appointments, managing calendars, and organizing files.
  • Communication Management: Handle emails, phone calls, and other correspondence on behalf of clients, ensuring timely responses.
  • Data Entry: Maintain and update databases and records with accurate and detailed information.
  • Research: Conduct online research to gather information and prepare reports or presentations as needed.
  • Task Coordination: Assist in coordinating projects and tasks, ensuring deadlines are met and priorities are managed effectively.
  • Social Media Management: Help manage social media accounts, including content creation and engagement with followers.
  • Client Interaction: Serve as a point of contact for clients, addressing inquiries and providing support as necessary.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience:
    - Previous experience as a virtual assistant or in a similar administrative role is preferred.
    - Proven ability to manage multiple tasks and projects simultaneously.
  • Skills:
    - Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with a professional demeanor.
    - Strong organizational abilities and attention to detail.
    - Proficiency in office software (e.g., Microsoft Office, Google Workspace) and familiarity with virtual collaboration tools.
    - Ability to anticipate needs and solve problems independently.
  • Education: A high school diploma or equivalent; a degree in a related field is a plus.
  • Location: Based in Addis Ababa with flexibility for hybrid or in-office work as needed.

Employee Benefits

  • Career Growth: Be part of a leading company with opportunities for advancement and professional development.
  • Impactful Role: Take on a pivotal role where your contributions directly influence the success of the team and organization.
  • Supportive Environment: Join a company that values your contributions and invests in your professional development.
  • Competitive Compensation: We offer a competitive salary and benefits package.
