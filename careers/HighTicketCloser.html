Job Description

(
High Ticket Closer
)

As a High Ticket Closer, you’ll be responsible for converting qualified leads into paying clients for premium products or services. You’ll use your sales expertise to build trust, uncover client needs, and confidently guide prospects toward making a purchase.

This is a full-time position with uncapped earning potential, perfect for someone who excels in high-performance sales.

Job Responsibilities

This is a full-time position offering the chance to grow your skills in a fast-paced, client-centric environment.

  • Close High-Ticket Sales: Conduct sales calls with qualified leads and close deals for high-value products or services.
  • Build Client Relationships: Develop rapport with prospects, understand their needs, and present tailored solutions.
  • Follow a Proven Sales Process: Use a structured approach to guide clients through the decision-making process.
  • Handle Objections: Confidently address questions or concerns to help clients make informed decisions.
  • Track Performance: Maintain accurate records of calls, conversions, and sales metrics.
  • Collaborate with Teams: Work closely with the marketing and lead-generation teams to optimize the sales funnel.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience:
    - At least 2-3 years of experience in high-ticket sales or closing deals over $2,000+.
    - A proven track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets.
  • Skills:
    - Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.
    - Confidence in conducting consultative sales calls and building trust with prospects.
    - Strong objection-handling and negotiation skills.
    - Ability to work independently and manage time effectively.
  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in business, communications, or a related field is preferred.
  • Location: Based in Addis Ababa with reliable internet for remote or hybrid work, if required.

Employee Benefits

  • Global Opportunities: Work with international clients and gain global exposure.
  • Career Growth: Be part of a leading BPO company with opportunities for advancement.
  • Supportive Environment: Join a company that values your contributions and invests in your professional development.
  • Competitive Compensation: We offer a competitive salary and benefits package.
